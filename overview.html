Overview of Hagrid

Rubeus Hagrid, often simply called Hagrid, is a character in the Harry Potter series. He is the Keeper of Keys and Grounds at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Despite his rugged appearance and towering stature, Hagrid is known for his kind and gentle nature.

Early Life

Hagrid's early life is shrouded in mystery. He was expelled from Hogwarts in his third year under suspicion of opening the Chamber of Secrets, though he was innocent. Albus Dumbledore, then the headmaster, allowed him to stay at Hogwarts as the Keeper of Keys and Grounds.

Magical Creatures

Hagrid has a deep love for magical creatures, from his loyal boarhound Fang to the dangerous Blast-Ended Skrewts. His passion for creatures often leads him into interesting and sometimes perilous situations, but his care for them is unwavering.
